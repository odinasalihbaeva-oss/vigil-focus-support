FAQ

How do I set my daily goal?
Open Vigil → tap "Set a goal" on the home screen.

How do I add widgets?
Long press your Home Screen → tap "+" → search "Vigil".

How do I enable Live Activities?
Go to Settings → Live Activities → tap to enable.

How do I reset my streak?
Streaks reset automatically if you miss a day.
